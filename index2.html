For Parents

Club Lingua is a great way for your child to learn a new language by playing fun and interactive games.

They can create their own profile and customise their avatar to use in the games.

They can hang out with their friends and practise their new language skills.

We want their language learning to be fun, with a safe community and a culture of friendship and creativity.

As a parent you can access your childs account from your own account, to monitor their progess and activity, for their safety and your peace of mind.
